Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs your use of the software application Goodbye Insomnia: Sleep Aid (the "Application") for mobile devices, which was created by KhayrApps (the "Developer").

The Application is a sleep aid utility designed to play audio sounds and provide relaxation tools. This Policy explains how information is collected, protected, and used within the Application.

1. Information Collection and Sharing

The Application does not feature a user registration system or account creation profile. We do not manually collect or store personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the Application uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to serve advertisements. The Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically collects and shares certain user data directly with Google's servers for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes.

This data may include:

  • Approximate Location: Your general IP-based location is processed to deliver advertisements relevant to your region or country.
  • App Interactions: Information about your interactions with advertisements, such as ad clicks, video views, and app launch frequency.
  • App Info and Performance: Technical data including crash logs and performance diagnostics used to improve application stability.
  • Device or Other IDs: Unique device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or App Set ID) used to manage advertising, reduce repetitive ads, and prevent fraud.

2. Encryption and Security

All information automatically transmitted by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using the industry-standard Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol. This helps protect data from unauthorized access or tampering during transmission.

3. Data Deletion

Because the Application does not provide user accounts, and advertising data is associated with anonymous device identifiers rather than personal profiles, the Developer does not offer a manual data deletion process.

You can manage or reset your device's advertising identifier at any time through your Android device by going to:

Settings > Privacy > Ads

4. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly target or collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information without your consent, please contact us.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the Last Updated date at the top of this Policy will be revised accordingly.
